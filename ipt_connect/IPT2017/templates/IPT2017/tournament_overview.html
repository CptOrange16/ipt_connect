Welcome!

This interface gathers the results of the 9th International Physicists' Tournament .

Most of the displayed element can be clicked on. You can access teams, individual participant or jury member statistics, as well as details of each rounds, physics fights and presented problem.

The results are updated as soon as possible but during the tournament the results on this interface may not be the official ones (calculation time and checking). Please check your emails and the website for official statements.

In case you spot a bug or have any suggestion, feel free to get in touch with us at webmaster (a) iptnet (dot) info.
